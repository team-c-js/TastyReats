Banana Pancakes

#Breakfast #Dessert #Sweet
⭐ 4.5 ⏱ 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1 large Banana
  • 2 medium Eggs
  • 1 tsp Oil
  • 25 tsp Pecan nuts
  • Pinch Baking Powder
  • 125 g Raspberries

Recipe

Mash the ripe banana in a medium mixing bowl. Add the egg and whisk together until well combined. Add the flour, baking powder, cinnamon (if using), and salt, and stir until just combined. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and add the oil. Scoop the batter and cook for 2–3 minutes per side until golden brown. Serve warm with toppings like syrup or fruit.
