BANANA PANCAKES

  • #Breakfast
  • #Desert
  • #Sweet
4.5
40 min
  • Banana 1 large
  • Eggs 2 medium
  • Oil 1 tsp
  • Pecan nuts 25 tsp
  • Baking Powder Pinch
  • Raspberries 125 g
Mash the ripe banana in a medium mixing bowl. Add the egg and whisk together until well combined. Add the flour, baking powder, cinnamon (if using), and salt, and stir until just combined.Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and add the vegetable oil or melted butter. Scoop the batter onto the skillet or griddle using a 1/4 cup measure, and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the pancakes are golden brown and cooked through. Serve warm with your favorite toppings such as maple syrup, sliced bananas, or whipped cream.
